Interesting Facts about chameleon

  • chameleons can see 360 degrees around them if they want to
  • Chameleons come in a lot of different shapes and sizes

Interesting Facts about panda

  1. Giant pandas spend 10-16 hours a day feeding, mainly on bamboo
  2. On average, pandas poo 40 times a day
  3. They have an extended wrist bone that they use like a thumb to help them grip food
